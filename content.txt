This is the best learning order for your MERN project.




1. User places order
        ↓
2. Order Service creates PENDING order
        ↓
3. Publish ORDER_CREATED
        ↓
4. Inventory Service consumes event
        ↓
5. Acquire Redis lock
        ↓
6. Check stock
        ↓
7. Reserve stock
        ↓
8. Release lock
        ↓
9. Publish STOCK_RESERVED
        ↓
10. Payment Service consumes event
        ↓
11. Process payment
        ↓
12A. Publish PAYMENT_SUCCESS
            OR
12B. Publish PAYMENT_FAILED
        ↓
13. Consumers react independently:
    ├── Order Service updates order status
    ├── Inventory confirms/releases reservation
    └── Notification sends email/SMS
